About Our Pahadi Ghar

Nestled in the serene landscapes of Darcha Dangma, The Pahadi Ghar-Darcha is more than just a homestay; it's an emotion. Experience authentic Lahauli hospitality where the air is pure and the stars are brighter. Our home offers panoramic views of the Bhaga River and the surrounding peaks.

— Hosted by Chhering Tashi and Thinleisss

Comfortable Stay & Home Cooked Food

Enjoy cozy rooms built with traditional stones and wood to keep you warm. Our kitchen serves farm-to-table organic Himachali meals, including Thukpa, Siddu, and fresh local vegetables.

How to Reach Darcha

By Road: Darcha is located on the Manali-Leh Highway. You can take a bus or taxi from Manali (approx. 100km via Atal Tunnel).

By Air: The nearest airport is Bhuntar (Kullu), about 150km away.

By Rail: The closest major station is Chandigarh, from where you can drive up to Lahaul Valley.
